# Mutual Fund Analytics Project                  Date: 24-06-26

## Project Overview

This project analyzes mutual fund industry data using Python and data visualization techniques. The objective is to identify trends in NAV performance, AUM growth, SIP inflows, investor behavior, folio growth, fund correlations, and sector allocations.

The analysis was performed using multiple datasets containing mutual fund schemes, NAV history, SIP inflows, investor transactions, portfolio holdings, and benchmark data.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze daily NAV trends across mutual fund schemes.
* Study AUM growth of major fund houses.
* Track SIP inflow growth over time.
* Examine category-wise fund inflows.
* Understand investor demographics and behavior.
* Analyze geographic distribution of investments.
* Study folio growth trends.
* Measure correlation among selected mutual funds.
* Evaluate sector allocation patterns.
* Generate business insights from the data.

## Analysis Performed

### Task 1: NAV Trend Analysis

* Plotted NAV trends for selected mutual fund schemes.
* Studied long-term growth patterns.

### Task 2: AUM Growth Analysis

* Compared Assets Under Management across fund houses.
* Identified leading AMCs based on AUM.

### Task 3: SIP Inflow Trend Analysis

* Analyzed monthly SIP inflows from 2022 to 2025.
* Highlighted record SIP inflow milestones.

### Task 4: Category-wise Inflow Heatmap

* Visualized category-level fund inflows using a heatmap.
* Compared investor preferences across categories.

### Task 5: Investor Demographics Analysis

* Studied age-group distribution of investors.
* Analyzed investment amounts across demographic groups.

### Task 6: Geographic Distribution Analysis

* Compared investment activity across states and cities.
* Evaluated participation from different regions.

### Task 7: Folio Growth Analysis

* Tracked growth in mutual fund folio counts.
* Measured expansion of the investor base.

### Task 8: Correlation Matrix Analysis

* Calculated correlations among selected mutual fund returns.
* Evaluated diversification opportunities.

### Task 9: Sector Allocation Analysis

* Analyzed portfolio exposure across sectors.
* Identified dominant investment sectors.

### Task 10: Key EDA Findings

* Summarized major business insights derived from the analysis.

---

## Key Findings

1. Mutual fund NAVs showed strong long-term growth.
2. SBI Mutual Fund maintained the highest AUM among leading fund houses.
3. SIP inflows steadily increased during the study period.
4. Investor participation expanded significantly between 2022 and 2025.
5. Mid-Cap and Flexi-Cap categories attracted strong inflows.
6. The majority of investors belonged to the working-age population.
7. Major cities contributed a significant share of investments.
8. Folio counts nearly doubled during the analysis period.
9. Large-cap funds exhibited high positive correlation.
10. Banking and IT sectors received substantial portfolio allocations.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates the use of Python-based data analytics techniques to analyze mutual fund industry trends and generate actionable insights. The analysis highlights growth in investor participation, increasing SIP adoption, strong industry expansion, and important portfolio allocation patterns.
