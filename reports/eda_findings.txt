# Mutual Fund Analytics - Key EDA Findings

### 1. NAV Trend Analysis

The NAV trends of mutual fund schemes showed steady long-term growth from 2022 to 2025, with periodic fluctuations 
reflecting market conditions.

### 2. AUM Analysis

Assets Under Management increased across major fund houses during the study period. SBI Mutual Fund consistently 
maintained the highest AUM, indicating strong investor trust and market leadership.

### 3. SIP Inflow Analysis

Monthly SIP inflows displayed a strong upward trend and reached a record ₹31,002 crore in December 2025, 
highlighting growing retail investor participation.

### 4. Category-wise Inflow Analysis

Equity-oriented categories such as Flexi Cap, Mid Cap, and Small Cap attracted higher investor inflows compared to other categories, 
indicating preference for growth-focused investments.

### 5. Investor Demographics

Most investors belonged to working-age groups. Investment participation was higher among individuals with stable 
income levels and long-term wealth creation goals.

### 6. Geographic Distribution

Investor activity was concentrated in major states and urban regions. However, participation from Tier-2 
and smaller cities also increased, showing broader market penetration.

### 7. Folio Growth Analysis

The total number of mutual fund folios increased from 13.26 crore in January 2022 to 26.12 crore in December 2025, 
reflecting significant growth in the investor base.

### 8. Correlation Matrix Analysis

The correlation matrix showed that many selected funds exhibited positive correlations in NAV returns, indicating similar 
responses to market movements and economic conditions.

### 9. Sector Allocation Analysis

Portfolio investments were distributed across multiple sectors, with certain sectors receiving larger allocations 
due to their growth potential and market performance.

### 10. Overall Industry Growth

The mutual fund industry demonstrated strong expansion during the analysis period, supported by increasing SIP inflows, 
rising folio counts, higher AUM, and growing investor participation.

## Conclusion

The analysis indicates that the mutual fund industry experienced healthy growth between 2022 and 2025. 
Increasing investor awareness, strong SIP contributions, expanding folio counts, and sustained fund performance contributed 
to the industry's overall success.
